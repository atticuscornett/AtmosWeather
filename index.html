Why Atmos Weather?

Total Control

Atmos Weather gives you deep control over what you see and how it behaves. Add as many locations as you want, customize notifications, and choose what information you want to see. Atmos Weather is designed to be flexible and powerful, so you can get the most out of your weather app.

Detailed Alerts

Atmos Weather provides detailed weather alerts for your locations, so you can stay informed about severe weather conditions. Alerts are displayed on a map with detailed information about the alert, including the type of alert, alert details, and instructions (if provided by the NWS).

Cross Platform

Atmos Weather is available on most platforms, including Android, Windows, macOS, Linux, and the web. This means you can use Atmos Weather on your phone, tablet, or computer, so you never miss a weather update. Whether you're at home, at work, or on the go, Atmos Weather is always there for you.

Privacy Focused

Atmos Weather is designed with privacy in mind. Your location data is only shared with services that are necessary for providing weather information. More information about what data Atmos Weather sends and why can be found in the Privacy Statement.

Open Source

Atmos Weather is open source software, which means that anyone can view the source code, contribute to the project, and help make it better. It also means that you can verify that Atmos Weather is not misusing your data. The source code is available on GitHub.

No Ads

Atmos Weather is completely ad-free. Atmos Weather is developed by volunteers and uses free services to provide weather information. This allows us to keep Atmos Weather free of ads and focused on providing the best weather experience possible.
