Important: Migration to Atmos Weather V2

Atmos Weather v2.0.0 is out now and brings big changes!

Atmos Weather v2 uses a different framework to run on mobile, and this means that Android users will have to reinstall Atmos Weather and re-add locations and settings to get the latest features.

This change does not affect desktop or web users.

Apologies for any inconvenience this causes mobile users. This should be a one time issue that does not affect future updates.

Atmos v2 features a completely revamped hourly forecast page that now shows more metrics, such as chance of precipitation. The radar page now features the ability the SPC outlooks for severe weather and other hazards. Numerous other quality of life improvements have been made, including Android widgets and an in-app theme switcher.

The future of weather alerts is here.

Atmos Weather is the ultimate lightweight weather forecast and alert app.
Unlike other apps, it isn't bloated with ads and doesn't put profit before user experience.
It's cross-platform, so you can get weather alerts on the go or at home.
Best of all, it's 100% free.
